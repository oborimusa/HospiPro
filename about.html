Our Story Meet the Team

Our Journey

From a simple idea to revolutionizing how hospitality businesses operate worldwide.

2018

The Beginning

Founded by hospitality veterans who saw the need for a unified platform that could streamline operations while enhancing guest experiences. Started with a small team of 5 passionate individuals.

2019

First Major Breakthrough

Launched our MVP with 50+ hotel partners. Secured $2M in seed funding to accelerate development and expand our team. Introduced our signature property management module.

2020

Pandemic Adaptation

Despite industry challenges, we pivoted to help hotels implement contactless solutions and remote management tools. Grew our customer base by 150% as businesses sought digital transformation.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded to 15 countries across Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Launched our tour management module and reached 1,000+ business customers. Recognized as "Most Innovative Hospitality Tech" by Industry Awards.

2024

Today & Beyond

Serving 2,500+ hospitality businesses worldwide with a team of 85+ dedicated professionals. Continuously innovating with AI-powered features and expanding our ecosystem to cover every aspect of hospitality and tourism.
