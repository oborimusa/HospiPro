Our Story: Transforming Hospitality in Africa

Discover how HospiPro is bridging the gap between tradition and technology in Africa's booming hospitality sector.

Welcome to HospiTour

HospiTour is a revolutionary online platform designed to empower hospitality businesses in Africa and beyond. Our mission is to provide a one-stop solution for hotels, restaurants, bars, event planners, and catering services to streamline operations, enhance customer relationships, and drive growth.

Founded in 2025, we aim to bridge the gap between tradition and technology in Africa's booming hospitality sector. We believe that by combining local expertise with cutting-edge technology, we can create sustainable growth opportunities for businesses across the continent.

Our team brings together decades of experience in hospitality management, technology development, and African market expertise to deliver solutions that truly understand and address the unique challenges faced by the industry.

Our Mission

To empower African hospitality businesses with innovative technology solutions that streamline operations, enhance guest experiences, and drive sustainable growth across the continent.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading hospitality technology ecosystem, connecting businesses, professionals, and guests in a seamless digital environment that celebrates African hospitality excellence.

Our Journey

From idea to impact - the story of HospiTour

The Beginning

Founded by hospitality veterans who saw the need for digital transformation in Africa's growing hospitality sector. Initial concept development and market research.

2023

Platform Development

Assembled a world-class tech team and began developing the core HospiPro platform with input from early adopter hotels and restaurants.

2024

Official Launch

Launched HospiTour with 50 founding business partners across Kenya, Nigeria, and South Africa. Received positive industry feedback and early traction.

2025

Expansion & Growth

Expanded to 10 African countries with over 500 business partners. Introduced new features including marketplace and professional networking.
