About HospiPro

Empowering Africa’s hospitality ecosystem with innovation and technology.

HospiPro is a revolutionary online platform designed to empower hospitality businesses in Africa and beyond. Our mission is to provide a one-stop solution for hotels, restaurants, bars, event planners, and catering services to streamline operations, enhance customer relationships, and drive growth.

Founded in 2025, we aim to bridge the gap between tradition and technology in Africa’s booming hospitality sector.
